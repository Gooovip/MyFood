Located in the heart of Amsterdam , we are a neighbourhood restaurant and café offering bright, contemporary dining in a relaxed, vibrant space. Made with love and the best of what’s in season, our dishes utilise produce from top British artisanal suppliers, with menus for breakfast, brunch, lunch and dinner, complemented by our bespoke coffee blend roasted in Naples and delivered weekly, elegant cocktails and freshly squeezed juices at our downstairs bar. With a focus on global flavours, good honest cooking and great service, LIV is all about quality, provenance and creating a sense of abundance – from generosity of spirit to the food on the plate.



MENUS

Designed for all-day grazing, our breakfast, lunch and dinner offerings include a set menu for kids and freshly baked goods, plus coffee, teas,
juices, smoothies, delicious cocktails, and a carefully selected list of global wines.
